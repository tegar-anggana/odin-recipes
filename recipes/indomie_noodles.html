Indomie Noodles

Description

Serve simple indomie noodles.

Ingredients

  • 1 Pack of Indomie Noodles
  • Water

Steps

  1. Boil some water in a pan.
  2. Put the noodles to it.
  3. Wait for about 3 minutes. While waiting, pour the seasoning into a plate.
  4. Put the noodles to the plate.
  5. Mix it with the seasoning and you're done!

You can add eggs or another seasonings if you like.
